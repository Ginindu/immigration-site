Services for Other Countries

Application Checklist (Selected Countries)

Study Visas (UK / Australia / NZ)
  • Valid passport and ID documents
  • Letter of acceptance from an educational institution
  • Proof of funds / scholarship letters
  • Academic transcripts and CV
  • Evidence of English language proficiency where required
Tourist / Visitor Visas
  • Confirmed travel itinerary and accommodation
  • Proof of ties to home country (employment, property)
  • Bank statements and travel insurance

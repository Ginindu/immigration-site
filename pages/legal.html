Legal Checklist

Documents to prepare for an appeal or review:

  • Copy of refusal or decision letter
  • All previously submitted application documents
  • New evidence or supporting material (e.g., medical, academic)
  • Detailed timeline and client statement
  • Contact information for witnesses or employers
  • Any prior correspondence with authorities
